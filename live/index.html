Kerby Ferris is an experimental sound artist, builder, and software engineer focused on Free and Open Sourced consciousness, interactive non-expert music making practices, and the feral delight of the unlikely surprise. Born and raised in Portland, Oregon, she has coded, collaborated and performed internationally in the clubs, museums, and community spaces of North America, Europe and Brazil.
